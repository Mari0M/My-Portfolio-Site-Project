My top 3 movies...

# 1 Interstellar

This is a movie about a space adventure where earth is running out of time and humans must find another planet or a way to save earth.

# 2 Searching for bobby fischer

This is a movie about a man who is trying to find bobby fischer who was a famous chess player at an extremely young age. He disapears from the chess world and was never found for many years.

# 3 Ender's Game

This is a movie about a boy who goes through a space military academy to become the captain of the space military. He believes he is saving his world from the evil aliens, but in reality he is murdering them all without his knowledge.
